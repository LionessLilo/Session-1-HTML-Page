Introduction

Welcome! I started crocheting, like many others, while searching for a relaxing hobby to keep my hands busy during screen time. Tired of mindless scrolling, I craved a creative outlet that could also combat stress. What began with simple stitches in front of the TV quickly blossomed into a full-fledged passion. Now, I'm excited to share this journey with you. Dive in, grab your hook, and let's get creating!
